Last Lab

Day 8 in New York City

Of course everybody was sad today because we were getting closer and closer to leaving camp, but we were definitely more excited than sad. Today was the last day we were doing a lab which meant that we started our final projects the next day. Before lunch, we all did our last lab which generated gifs. In preparation for the next day, we finished our lunch and got with our partners. I was fortunate enough to be teamed up with one of my closest friends at camp, Joanna. We had gotten together and started to brainstorm. We came up with three potential ideas for a webapp and ended up choosing a website that generates ten best fitting books based off of a genre type. Joanna and I were excited toget started on the webapp and watch it come to life.
