First Group Project

Day 5 in New York City

It was our first out of two fridays at Kode With Klossy! I walked in and saw that Sarah brought in donuts for all of us with was a great start to our busy morning. Today was the day for our first group project. After our warmups and circle time, we sat down and got assigned to our partners. I was thrilled ot be matched up with Lenor and Bernice because they were really nice and funny.

We were all assigned to code a calculator that could add, subract, etc., but after that we had the freedom to get creative and create a calculator that calculated anything we wanted. Bernice, Lenor, and I had come up with an idea for a calculator that generated gifs depending on the inserted emotion.

We came up with something very cool and we were very proud of our product that we finished successfully. During presentations, we felt really glad when we typed in an emotion that we inserted into the code, and the computer returned a funny gif that was related to the given emotion.
