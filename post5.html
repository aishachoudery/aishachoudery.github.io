First Group Project

Day 5 in New York City

I walked into the WeWork tower feeling excited, and ready to learn something new, but what I felt the most was nervousness. I saw a group of girls standing near the door and knew that they were there for the same reason I was. I was shy and felt like it was going to be extremely hard to find friends. The ta from level two took us to the sixth floor and into the room where the magic happened. I walked in and saw everyone walking around and talking, so I sat at a table where there were four other girls. We started talking, then talked some more, and I realized that everyone else was just as nervous as I was. I was shocked that I had already made a great group of friends on my first day. We were having a great time talking, but then we were called together to huddle in a circle and play some extremely fun improv games. Of course I was iffy about them at first, but I really grew to love them. After circle time, it was time for our class to start. We learned the basic foundation for Ruby and on that day I really realized my passion for computer programming.
