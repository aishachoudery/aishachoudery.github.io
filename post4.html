Karlie Kloss!

Day 4 in New York City

Thursday was a hectic day. We were told that we were going to have a guest speaker, the CEO for a feminine product company, Lola. We were learning object orientation in the first half of the day which I was pretty fond of . I remember at lunch time I finished my lunch and started socializing with my friends. Suddenly, the Victoria's Secret Angel Karlie Kloss walked through the door and everyone froze. Everybody was shocked and excited. After Karlie introduced herself to us, we started preparing for our guest speakers. Finally, when Jordana Kier came to talk to us about her company run by women, we were all very engaged in the conversation and proud that women took the lead in something that is usually run by men. We were also introduced to the story of Microsoft by Avi Flombaum. I still remember that day vividly because it was an amazing one.
