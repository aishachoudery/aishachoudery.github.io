About Me

This is who I am

Hey everyone! My name is Aisha Choudery and I am from Maryland. It was around the end of my eighth grade year when my brothers introduced me to computer science. I started by learning Python and once I got a hang of it, it really stuck to me. I learned about Kode With Klossy and I was really excited to apply for such a great oppurtunity. I applied, and a few months later, I got my acceptance letter. I ran around excited, then I started planning for the summer.

Now, I am working with my teacher to create an all girls coding club for my very own high school next year. With my school being so small, most of the young women don't even know what computer science could do for them. I am optimistic that my club will endorse computer science and help the coder community in Salisbury.

Not only will I always remember everything I learned from the amazing teachers, I will also remember the amazing women that I was surrounded by for the two weeks. As a Kode With Klossy alumna, I can say with certainty that Kode With Klossy was definitely an unforgetable experience!
