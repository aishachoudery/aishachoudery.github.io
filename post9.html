We Started Our Final Projects!

Day 9 in New York City

Our fist days starting our final projects was a nerve-racking day. I remember walking into the room in WeWork Tower 49 and getting straight to work. I sat next to my partner Joanna and we started to find a template on StartBootstrap.com. We found one that really stood out to us and look apprpoiate for our idea. We wanted our book generating web app to look formal and attractive.

The day went by, and we encountered so many errors that'd we'd lost count. Even though it got frustrated at times, every error equalled a lesson. We learned more and more as we tried new things and we took help from Melissa, Sarah, Carson, and Sam whenever we couldn't get around something. Of course we felt the most happiness when we fixed an error on our own.

The day was coming to the end, but our website was not even close. We felt the stress rain down upon us, but realized that we had half of a day to finish up the next day. Joanna and I still decided to collaborate on it at home by texting and helping each other. We hadn't gotten the chance to get help on adding an API in camp, but my brother was kind enough to help Joanna and I through it. It was great to walk into camp the next and only having to clean up a few things before the big Demo Day.
