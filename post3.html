Arrays, Hooray!

Day 3 in New York City

I walked into WeWork Tower getting ready to start coding on a Wednesday morning. We sat down and started on our warmups. Our warmups were to create an array with a list of our favorite artists. It was very fun being able to share my love for music with my partner Jaylene.

After warmups, as we usually did, we got into our large circle and started the day with either a game or a stretch activity. I always looked forward to circle time because it was a great way to take a break, get together, and just share some laughs.

After circle time we got back to arrays. Arrays are basically ordered collections of any object you choose. Lunchtime came, and we had an amazing meal as always. After lunch we did another lab with arrays. We gathered in our "End Of The Day Circle" and Lenor had come up with a cheer for us. We all put our hands in the middle and said "New Kloss City!" on three. I went home feeling smarter than the day before.
