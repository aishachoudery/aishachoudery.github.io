Our Last "New Kloss City"

Day 10 in New York City

September 1st for me was both Eid and Kode With Klossy Demo Day. Everyone was extremely excited to finish their projects and present them, but also super sad at the same time because it was time to say good-bye. We started the day by working on finishing our final projects. The morning was filled with a little stress but mostly success. When the time finally came, we finally uploaded our websites on Heroku.app.com, high fives for everyone, and we gathered in our circle for the last time. We gave our final shoutouts to everyone who made the past two weeks amazing. Shoutouts came to an end and we cheered for the last time “New Kloss City!” We set up for Demo Day and everyone’s guests arrived. We’d gotten through all of the website presentations and set-up our computers for the behind-the-scenes code. Now was time for graduation. Everybody was called up to get a diploma and some Kode With Klossy swag and when graduation was over, we all talked and said our goodbyes. I got to spend plenty of time with my friends, but I had to head to my Aunt’s house to celebrate Eid with my family. It was the most amazing two weeks all summer and I will never forget the extraordinary people, teachers, and opportunities that Kode With Klossy gave me. Until next summer New Kloss City!
